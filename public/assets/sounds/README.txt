Place your sound effect files here. Recommended filenames:
- next.mp3: Play when advancing to the next question
- reveal.mp3: Play when revealing an answer
- select.mp3: Play when selecting a question on the board
- correct.mp3: Play for correct answer feedback
- incorrect.mp3: Play for incorrect answer feedback

You can use .mp3 or .wav files. Update the code if you use different names.
